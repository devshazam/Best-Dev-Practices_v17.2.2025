        - https://medium.com/@shuvo_tdr/testing-next-js-applications-with-jest-a-comprehensive-guide-48cffa37110b

        - `./templates/jest.config.ts`
    3_ `packege,json` - внести 
          "scripts": {
                "test": "jest",
                "test:watch": "jest --watch"
